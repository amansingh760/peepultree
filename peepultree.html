Product Description ▾
Originating from the Kutch region of Gujarat, Lippan art is an ancient wall decoration technique using a mixture of mud and camel dung, now adapted with clay on wood. Skilled artisans press tiny convex mirrors into intricate geometric and floral patterns, catching light to create a mesmerizing ripple effect throughout the day.

This panel features a classic red-and-black mandala motif — 12 layers of radiating lotus petals framed by a bold border of dancing peacocks. Each mirror is hand-set and each clay ridge individually shaped, making every piece one of a kind. No two panels are exactly alike.
